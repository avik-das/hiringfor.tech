The weekly newsletter for all things tech hiring

Tech interviews are hard, whether you're hiring or looking to be hired. Poor interview practices mean the right candidates are not being matched with the right companies. In this weekly newsletter, I'll send you advice on how to prepare for an interview, as a candidate or as an interviewer.
